FuelWise — Figma Design Prompt
Design a mobile app called FuelWise. The app is a gas and EV station finder with trip planning. Use the following color palette exclusively throughout all screens:
Primary / Action: F15025
Background: FFFFFF
Surface / Card: E6E8E6
Border / Divider: CED0CE
Text / Dark: 191919
All screens are designed for a standard mobile frame at 390x844px. Typography uses a clean sans-serif system font. Body text is 14-16px. Headings are 18-22px. Labels and captions are 12px. Font weight is regular (400) for body and medium (500) for headings and labels. No font weight heavier than 500. All text is sentence case, no all-caps. Line height is 1.5 for body text. The primary action color F15025 is used for all interactive elements including buttons, active states, icons, and key data highlights. Neutral surfaces use E6E8E6. Borders use CED0CE at 0.5-1px. All text on white or light surfaces uses 191919. White FFFFFF text is used only when placed on the F15025 primary color.
The border radius is 12px for cards and containers, 8px for inputs and smaller components, and 999px for pill-shaped tags and badges.
Design the following screens in order:
Screen 1 — Login. Full white background. Centered layout. App name FuelWise in large 28px medium weight using 191919. Tagline below in 14px CED0CE. Below that, an email input field and a password input field, both with 1px CED0CE border, 12px radius, 16px internal padding, and 191919 placeholder text. Below the inputs, a full-width Sign In button with F15025 background, white text, and 12px radius. Below that, a smaller text link in 14px F15025 that reads "Create an account". Minimal layout, generous vertical spacing.
Screen 2 — Getting Started. White background. Top section has a progress indicator showing step 1 of 1. A heading reads "Tell us about your vehicle". Below are two input fields: Car Model and Model Year, both styled identically to the login inputs. Below that, a row labeled "Fuel type" with two selectable pill options: Gas and Electric. The selected pill is filled F15025 with white text; the unselected is E6E8E6 background with 191919 text and CED0CE border. Below that, a section labeled "Current energy level" with five selectable segments representing Empty, 1/4, 1/2, 3/4, and Full. The segments are arranged in a horizontal row, each as a rounded rectangle. The selected segment fills with F15025. Below the segments, a small caption in 12px CED0CE reads "This helps us estimate your range". At the bottom of the screen, a full-width Continue button in F15025 with white text.
Screen 3 — Service Selection. White background. Top bar has a small circular profile avatar icon on the right aligned to the top. The greeting text reads "Good morning" in 14px 191919 and a subline in 12px CED0CE shows the registered car name. Below, a section heading in 18px 191919 reads "What do you need today?". Three option cards are stacked vertically. Each card has E6E8E6 background, CED0CE border, 12px radius, 16px padding. Card one reads "Find Nearest Station" with a small label below in 12px CED0CE. Card two reads "Find Cheapest Station". Card three reads "Plan a Trip". Each card has a right-pointing chevron icon on the right side in CED0CE. At the bottom of the screen, a floating fuel gauge component shows the current vehicle fuel level as a horizontal bar. The filled portion uses F15025. The bar sits in an E6E8E6 container with a label showing the estimated remaining range in miles.
Screen 4 — Find Nearest. White background. At the top is a search bar with a range input field showing "Within 10 miles" and a small filter icon on the right in 191919. Below the search bar, a full-width map placeholder occupies roughly half the screen height. It has E6E8E6 fill and a centered label in 14px CED0CE reading "Map view". Station pins on the map use F15025 fill with white text showing price or distance. Below the map, a scrollable list of station result cards. Each card is white with CED0CE border, 12px radius, and 12px internal padding. Each card shows the station name in 14px 191919 medium weight, the distance in 12px CED0CE, and a small row of filter tags as pills using E6E8E6 background and 191919 text. If a station requires a membership, a small pill tag in F15025 background with white text reads "Members only". A floating circular button in the bottom right has F15025 background, white icon, and represents the AI chatbot.
Screen 5 — Find Cheapest. Identical structure to Screen 4 with the following differences. The search bar input reads "Within 5 mile radius". The station result cards are sorted by price and each card prominently displays the price per gallon or per kWh in F15025 at 18px medium weight. A small "Last updated" timestamp in 12px CED0CE appears below the price. The list is ordered cheapest first.
Screen 6 — Trip Planner. White background. A heading reads "Plan your trip". Two input fields are stacked: one labeled "Starting point" defaulting to "Current location" and one labeled "Destination". Both use the standard input style. Below the inputs, two large selectable cards sit side by side. Left card reads "Manual" with a description below in 12px CED0CE. Right card reads "AI-assisted". The selected card has a F15025 border at 2px and a subtle F15025 tint at 5 percent opacity on the background. The unselected card uses standard E6E8E6 fill with CED0CE border. Below the mode cards, a section labeled "Planned stops" shows a vertical list of stop cards. Each stop card is E6E8E6 background with a small station icon, stop number, station name, and estimated fuel level on arrival shown as a mini horizontal bar. A plus icon button in F15025 allows the user to add a stop manually. A full-width Start Trip button in F15025 with white text sits at the bottom.
Screen 7 — Trip In Action. This is the active navigation view. The upper two-thirds of the screen is occupied by a map placeholder in E6E8E6 with a route line drawn in F15025. A small vehicle position dot also uses F15025. The bottom third is a panel with white background and CED0CE top border. The panel heading reads "Upcoming stop" in 14px 191919 medium. Below is a station card showing name, miles until stop, current projected fuel level as a mini bar, and a small station detail row with price and amenities. To the right of the card is a Navigate button in F15025 background with white text at 14px. Below the station card, a horizontal scrollable list of further upcoming stops appears as compact pills showing stop number and estimated fuel on arrival.
Screen 8 — Profile. White background. At the top, a user avatar circle in E6E8E6 with initials centered in 191919. Below the avatar, the user name in 18px 191919 medium. A highlighted metric card below shows "Total saved" with a value in F15025 at 22px medium weight. Below that, a section heading reads "My vehicles". A card for each registered vehicle shows the make and model in 14px 191919, the year in 12px CED0CE, and a horizontal fuel gauge bar with the filled portion in F15025. A circular plus button in F15025 sits to the right of the section heading to add a vehicle. Below the vehicles section, a section heading reads "Favorite locations" with a list of saved station cards in the standard card style. Below that, a section heading reads "Journey history" with a compact list of past trips showing date, destination, and amount saved in F15025.
All screens share a consistent bottom navigation bar with four icon tabs: Home, Nearest, Cheapest, and Profile. Active tab icon and label use F15025. Inactive tabs use CED0CE. The bar background is FFFFFF with a 0.5px CED0CE top border.
All interactive states: buttons darken F15025 by 10 percent on press. Input fields get a 2px F15025 border on focus. Tappable cards get a slight E6E8E6 to CED0CE background shift on press.
